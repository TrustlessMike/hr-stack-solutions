# Best HRIS for Dental Practices 20-50 Employees

Dental practices with 20-50 employees need HR software that handles dental assistant scheduling, hygienist rotations, and multi-location coordination. This guide identifies the best HRIS platforms specifically for growing dental groups and DSOs.

## Why Dental Practice HR Differs from General Healthcare

Dental practices have unique HR requirements:

- **Clinical scheduling:** Hygienists book patient appointments 2-3 weeks out
- **Assistant scheduling:** DAs support multiple providers simultaneously
- **Production tracking:** Hygienist and DA compensation tied to production
- **Sterilization compliance:** OSHA infection control training and certification
- **Radiation certification:** X-ray certification tracking by state
- **Multi-location:** Dental groups operate 2-10 practice locations
- **Front desk scheduling:** Patient coordination requires extended coverage
- **Provider types:** General dentists, specialists, hygienists, DAs, front desk

Generic healthcare HRIS platforms miss dental-specific workflows.

## Critical Features for Dental Practice HRIS

### Hygienist Production Tracking

Hygienist compensation often ties to production. Your system needs:
- Daily production tracking by hygienist
- Collection percentage calculation
- Production bonus automation
- Integration with practice management (Dentrix, Eaglesoft)
- Production vs. goal reporting
- Hygienist productivity dashboards

### Dental Assistant Scheduling

DAs support multiple providers. Require:
- Operatory-based scheduling
- Provider-to-DA assignment tracking
- Sterilization rotation management
- Cross-training tracking
- Float DA management across locations
- Integration with patient scheduling

### Credential Tracking for Dental Staff

Dental certifications require tracking:
- Radiation certification by state
- CPR/BLS certification expiration
- Infection control training (annual)
- OSHA bloodborne pathogens training
- Dental assisting state licenses
- Expanded function certifications (state-specific)
- Continuing education tracking

### Multi-Location Dental Group Management

Dental groups operate multiple practices. Your HRIS needs:
- Cost center tracking by location
- Location-specific scheduling rules
- Transfer tracking between practices
- Location-based production reporting
- Multi-location org chart
- Location-specific onboarding checklists
- Regional manager dashboards

### Front Desk and Patient Coordination Scheduling

Front desk requires extended coverage. Ensure:
- Extended hours scheduling (early mornings, evenings)
- Phone coverage rotation
- Patient coordination scheduling
- Insurance verification coverage
- Multi-location phone routing
- After-hours on-call scheduling

## Dental Practice Compliance Risks

Common audit triggers for dental practices:

### Credential Expiration
- **Risk:** Hygienist or DA practicing with expired radiation certification
- **Penalty:** State dental board fines, potential license suspension
- **Prevention:** Automated alerts 60-90 days before expiration

### OSHA Compliance
- **Risk:** Missing infection control or bloodborne training
- **Penalty:** $15,000+ per serious violation
- **Prevention:** Automated training tracking and reminders

### Production Compensation Errors
- **Risk:** Incorrect hygienist production bonus calculation
- **Penalty:** Back pay + employee relations issues
- **Prevention:** Automated production tracking with practice management integration

### Multi-State Compliance
- **Risk:** DSO operating across states without proper compliance
- **Penalty:** State labor law violations, tax penalties
- **Prevention:** Multi-state HRIS with automatic compliance updates

## Dental Practice HRIS Selection Framework

Follow this decision framework to choose the right platform:

### Step 1: Count Your Provider Types
- **Single dentist:** Gusto or BambooHR sufficient

### Step 2: Map Your Location Structure
- **Single location:** Any platform works
- **2-3 locations:** Prioritize multi-location features

### Step 3: Audit Your Compensation Complexity
- **Salary only:** Basic payroll sufficient
- **Hygienist production:** Require production tracking integration

## Dental Credential Tracking Guide

Follow this guide for complete dental credential compliance:

### Dental Credential Categories
1. **Dentist Licensure:**
   - DDS/DMD license by state
   - DEA registration
   - Controlled substance license (state-specific)
   - Board certification status (if specialist)

2. **Hygienist Licensure:**
   - RDH license by state
   - Local anesthesia certification
   - Nitrous oxide certification
   - Continuing education tracking

3. **Dental Assistant Certification:**
   - CDA (Certified Dental Assistant)
   - RDA (Registered Dental Assistant) by state
   - X-ray certification by state
   - Infection control certification

4. **Specialist Credentials:**
   - Endodontics board certification
   - Orthodontics board certification
   - Oral surgery board certification
   - Periodontics board certification

5. **Compliance Training:**
   - OSHA bloodborne pathogens (annual)
   - Infection control (annual)
   - Radiation safety (state-specific)
   - HIPAA training (annual)

### Credential Alert Workflow
1. **120 days out:** Initial renewal reminder to provider
2. **90 days out:** Office manager notification
3. **60 days out:** Escalation to practice administrator
4. **30 days out:** Suspension workflow initiated
5. **Expired:** Practice management system flag

### Credential Documentation Requirements
- Primary source verification
- Copy of current license
- DEA certificate (if applicable)
- Board certification certificate
- Malpractice declarations page
- CE completion certificates
- X-ray certification (for DAs)

## Dental Production Tracking Guide

Hygienist and DA compensation often ties to production. Configure for your model:

### Hygienist Compensation Models
1. **Base + Commission:**
   - Base salary: Fixed hourly or salary
   - Commission: % of hygiene production
   - Threshold: Minimum production for commission
   - True-up: Monthly or quarterly reconciliation

2. **Pure Commission:**
   - Commission rate: 30-35% of hygiene production
   - Minimum guarantee: Floor for slow months
   - Draw against commission: Advance recovery
   - Reconciliation: Monthly calculation

3. **Hourly + Bonus:**
   - Hourly rate: Fixed hourly wage
   - Production bonus: % over threshold
   - Collection bonus: % of collected amounts
   - Quality bonus: Patient satisfaction metrics

### Dental Assistant Compensation Models
1. **Hourly + Production:**
   - Hourly rate: Fixed hourly wage
   - Production bonus: % of chair production
   - Procedure bonus: Specific procedure incentives
   - Team bonus: Practice-wide performance

2. **Salary + Bonus:**
   - Base salary: Fixed annual amount
   - Production bonus: Quarterly production targets
   - Collection bonus: A/R performance
   - Quality bonus: Patient satisfaction

### Dentist Compensation Models
1. **Salary + Collections:**
   - Base salary: Fixed annual amount
   - Collections bonus: % over threshold
   - Quality bonus: Production metrics
   - Leadership stipend: Management duties

2. **Pure Collections:**
   - Collections rate: 25-30% of collections
   - Minimum guarantee: Floor for slow months
   - Lab fee deduction: Lab cost offset
   - Reconciliation: Monthly calculation
